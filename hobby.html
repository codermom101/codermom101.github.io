In this page, I want to talk about a simple recipe that you can start on if you are new to cooking. Trust me and try it out and it will be a fun and relaxing experience. Today, we are going to look at kids' favorite "Chicken Alfredo pasta". To make it beginner friendly, I am not making Pasta and Sauce in this recipe, but rather using store bought ones. This way, your first experience of cooking is simple and straightforward.

  • First, melt some butter on a pan and fry (cut pieces) garlic slightly.
  • Once you get the smell of garlic and butter, add chicken.
  • Cook until the chicken is completely done well on medium flame.
  • Usually it takes around 3 - 5 minutes. Once chicken is cooked, add required seasoning that satisfies your taste buds.
  • Then, add Alfredo sauce to the chicken and give it a quick mix.
  • Add the Penna tubes and completely and rest it on the stove for 5 min.

Finally, serve it on a plate with garlic bread.
